How to Make a Sandwich:

  1. Get bread, peanut butter, strawberry jam, and nutella
  2. Put the peanut butter on one piece of bread and put the jam and nutella on the other
  3. Spread it out so you have an abundance of peanut butter, but not that much jam and nutella
  4. Gently put the two pieces together
  5. Cut off the crusts then cut it in the shape of a right triangle
  6. Enjoy!
